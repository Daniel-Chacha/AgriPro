Guidance on Improving Farm Eligibility for Support

1. Maintain Accurate Records: Keep detailed records of farm operations, expenses, and income.

2. Build a Strong Business Plan: Outline your farm's goals, financial projections, and strategies for growth.

3. Engage with Community Programs: Participate in local agricultural and community events to strengthen your network and credibility.

4. Stay Informed: Keep up-to-date with new funding opportunities and policy changes affecting the agricultural sector.
